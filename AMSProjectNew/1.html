1) Drink Water
 
What to do right now: Drink 16 ounces of water
What to do every day: Drink ½ your body weight in ounces of water
 
– Formula –
Ex: Weight of 200 pounds x (1/2) = 100 ounces
Your weight _____________ x (1/2) = __________________ ounces
 
Why?
Health benefits:
Increases energy
Gets rid of headaches
Burns fat
Eliminates food cravings
Helps alkalize your body
 
 
 
2) Breathe
 
What to do right now: Take 10 deep lower belly breaths. Here is a cadence to practice. Place your tongue on the roof of your mouth behind your teeth for the chi (energy) breath. As you breathe in through your nose, your belly extends outward for 4 seconds – 2 second hold – 8 seconds out through your mouth as your belly is relaxed back inward.
 
What to do every day: Practice this type of breathing a few times a day until it becomes habitual. I suggest setting alarms on your phone as a reminder. Eventually it will become unconscious and you will naturally breathe this way.
 
Why?
Health benefits:
Increases energy
Releases endorphins (Neurotransmitters that make you FEEL GOOD)
Improves overall cell function
Releases toxins from your body
Releases tension
Promotes fat loss
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
3) The Energizer
 
What to do right now:
 
Step 1: Identify what emotions or feelings you need more of right now.
Step 2: Write down a statement that represents what you presently need.
Ex: I am powerful, confident and ready to create massive, positive change with the work I’m doing.
Step 3: If you can… yell this statement with tons of emotion and passion
Step 4: As you say this statement follow it up by doing your signature celebration move.
Ex: A celebration move that a football player may do after scoring.
Step 5: Celebrate like your team just won the championship
What to do every day:
Practice the energizer move whenever you need a boost that will get you back into your optimal state of mind.
 
Why?
Health Benefits:
Improves blood flow throughout body
Boosts energy
Improves mood
Reduces Stress
 
 
 
 
 
 
4) Eat
 
What to do right now: If you haven’t eaten in 3 hours, then grab a healthy snack. A healthy snack could include some green veggies or healthy fats such as almonds or hemp seeds.
 
What to do every day:
- Try to eat healthy meals every 3 hours, when possible
- Eat a balanced meal with all 3 macronutrients (carbohydrates, fats, and proteins). Make sure to load up on some green veggies, as well.
- Eat a moderate portion to keep you energized, but not stuffed to the point that you’re ready for a nap.
 
Why?
Health benefits:
Increases energy
Improves mood
Helps alkalize your body
Boosts metabolism speed
Increases muscle mass
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
5) Meditate
 
What to do right now: Close your eyes and do a quick three- minute meditation. Instructions: Take five deep belly breaths, close your eyes and relax your shoulders. Visualize yourself three years from now living your dream life. Step into your body three years from now, where you’re enjoying the feelings of having everything that you have ever desired. Bask in the beautiful life you’re living and then open your eyes with newfound inspiration and optimism.
 
What to do every day: I would suggest doing the above described meditation every morning before you do anything else. This meditation will put you in an awesome “success” mind state to start your day.
 
Why?
Health benefits
You’ll be attracting the life you desire
Increases energy
Improves mood
Boosts your immune system
Lowers your blood pressure
